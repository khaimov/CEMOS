## Project Vision
To move CE Managers from reactive "blocker-clearing" to proactive "outcome-engineering" by unifying customer intelligence, team capabilities, and execution velocity into a single AI-orchestrated interface.

## Executive Summary
In 2026, the volume of cloud-native workloads requires a management layer that automates the "administrative overhead" of leadership. Nexus 2026 provides a real-time pulse on both the **external environment** (customer news/market shifts) and **internal capacity** (team skills/delivery progress) to ensure successful cloud transformation outcomes.

## Target User Persona
* **Primary:** Outcome Customer Engineering (CE) Manager.
* **Goal:** Accelerate value realization and move customers from agreement to consumption as quickly as possible.

## Key Functional Pillars

### A. The Strategic Customer Pulse (External Intelligence)
Monitoring the health and opportunities of the customer portfolio.
* **News & Sentiment Engine:** Real-time scraping of global news for assigned customers.
* **Roadblock Predictor:** Uses cross-functional data to flag potential roadblocks.
* **Outcome Dashboard:** Tracks progress against delivery plans.

### B. The Dynamic Talent Matrix (Internal Intelligence)
Focuses on team culture, talent strategy, and skills development.
* **LinkedIn-Synced Skills Matrix:** Automatically updates team profiles.
* **Gap Analysis AI:** Compares skills against customer pipeline requirements.
* **Career Growth Tracker:** Manages performance and career development.

### C. The Velocity Engine (Execution)
Drives the "delivery process" and revenue forecasting.
* **Consumption Forecasting:** Predictive modeling for revenue.
* **Executive Presentation Builder:** Generates strategic summaries.

## Success Metrics
* **Velocity Increase:** Reduction in time to consumption.
* **Talent Growth:** 20% increase in validations/certifications.
* **Forecast Accuracy:** Improved revenue prediction.
